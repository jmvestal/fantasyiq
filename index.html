      Privacy Policy
      Last updated: May 2026

      1. What We Collect
      When you sign in with Google, we store your name, email address, and a unique account ID. If you connect a fantasy league, we store your league settings, roster information, and trade history. We do not collect payment information.

      2. How We Use It
      Your data is used solely to provide TradeIQ features — syncing your settings across devices, saving connected leagues, and personalizing trade analysis. We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

      3. Third-Party Services
      TradeIQ uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, and the Sleeper API to fetch player and league data. We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate. These services have their own privacy policies.

      4. Data Storage
      Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with access restricted to your account only. League settings and preferences are also stored locally on your device via localStorage.

      5. Your Rights
      You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. You can also use the app as a guest (no sign-in required) — guest data is stored only on your device.

      6. Cookies
      TradeIQ does not use advertising cookies. We use localStorage for app functionality (caching rankings, saving settings). Firebase may set session cookies for authentication.

      7. Contact
      Questions? Email us at vestalagencyllc@gmail.com
